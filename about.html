About us

About this platfrom

This platform was built with a simple idea to make finding a
therapist feel a little less overwhelming. No confusing steps, no endless searching.
Just a clean space where you can reach out, book a session,
and take the first step toward feeling better.
It’s quiet, simple, and meant for real people like us.

What this platfrom is about

Each therapist profile comes with their availability
displayed in a clear, easy-to-use calendar format.
You choose the date and time that fits your schedule, book it in seconds, and that’s it.
No awkward calls. No long forms.
We wanted to keep it smooth and stress-free, the way it should be.

How this helps

We know what it feels like to want to talk but
not know where to begin. That’s why this platform exists to make support feel accessible and real.
Whether you're going through something tough or just want someone to talk to, this space is for you.
No pressure. Just help when you need it.

How it works

All it takes is a few clicks. Browse therapists, pick one that feels
right, check their schedule, and book your session.
You’ll receive reminders, and everything stays confidential.
We’ve handled the technical part
so you can focus on what really matters — your wellbeing.

Behind the scenes - About the developers

This project was executed by a group of students assigned to work together.
Collaboration was conducted primarily through shared documents and minimal communication.
Individual contributions were merged to form a cohesive output.
No interpersonal bonding was involved in the making of this platform.
